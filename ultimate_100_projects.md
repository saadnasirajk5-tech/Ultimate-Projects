# The Ultimate 100: From Zero to God-Tier AI/ML & Data Engineering Portfolio

## Introduction: The Blueprint for a World-Class Career

This document is a meticulously crafted blueprint for the aspiring **AI/ML Researcher**, **Machine Learning Engineer**, and **Data Engineer** who is targeting the most coveted roles at FAANG and top-tier remote companies. In the rapidly evolving landscape of 2026, a successful portfolio must demonstrate not just theoretical knowledge, but the ability to build **scalable, production-ready, and architecturally sound systems**.

We have structured this guide into four progressive tiers, comprising 100 projects. Each project is a deliberate learning experience, designed to:

1.  **Demonstrate Architectural Thinking**: Transitioning your skills from simple scripts to robust, distributed systems.
2.  **Utilize the 2026 Tech Stack**: Leveraging the latest in LLMs, Agentic AI, Real-time Data Fabrics, and modern cloud services.
3.  **Build a "Remote-Ready" Portfolio**: Showcasing your independence and ability to deliver complex, end-to-end solutions.

By completing these projects, you will not only master the technical tools but also cultivate the **strategic mindset** of a top-tier engineer, capable of articulating the business value and technical complexity of your work in any interview.
